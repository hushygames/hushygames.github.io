Last Day On Earth Survival

In this large ruined city, you have 51 locations to discover. When you collect some wood and metal, you can make weapons on the Workbench. When you win enough experience and level up, you can check your Attribute and upgrade HP, ATK, Speed, and Search. In the day, explore and go to places you have never visited. I hope you will survive 50 days and explore all 51 locations.
